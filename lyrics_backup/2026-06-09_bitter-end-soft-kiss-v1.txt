[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
You pull me closer in the fading light
We trace the shadows falling through the night
A tender secret burning in your eyes
The quiet morning feels a million miles

[Verse 1]
The wooden floors keep softly whispering
Your silhouette is all I am gathering
We left the busy world a mile away
Just wrapped inside the words we didn't say

[Pre-Chorus]
My fingers pull you closer to the fire
A quiet spark is waking up desire
The heavy skies are sleeping at the door
I only want to hold you even more

[Chorus]
It's a bitter end soft kiss that we share
With the taste of your memory in the air
I am holding the warmth of your skin tonight
We are lost in the glow of the amber light

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, your bitter end soft kiss
[Echo] (Bitter end soft kiss)

[Verse 2]
I watch the way you let defenses down
We are the only lovers in this town
The ticking clock is fading out of sight
As we surrender to the deep of night

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, your bitter end soft kiss
[Echo] (Bitter end soft kiss)

[Bridge]
It's a bitter end soft kiss, pulling me in
Where the memory ends and the night begins
Every single breath is waking up the heat
We are finding a rhythm in this quiet street
Just Saeed giving you a promise I will keep
While the rest of the lonely world goes to sleep

[Spoken]
[Whispered] (Just you and me now, leaving the world behind)
[Whispered] (You are the only truth I ever want to find)
